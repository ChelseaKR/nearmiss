Report a near-miss

A close pass, a door zone, a blind corner, a surface hazard — the close calls that never reach a police report. It takes under a minute. Every field except location is optional.

Your privacy

  • We ask for no name, email, account, or phone number — and please keep them out of the note too.
  • Your submission is reviewed by a person before it counts. Nothing you send appears on the map as-is.
  • We publish only aggregated street-block rates: never your exact spot, never the time, never any identifier. Low-count blocks are withheld entirely.
  • Full detail: docs/THREAT-MODEL.md and docs/SUBMISSIONS.md.
