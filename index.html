The primary artifact

A Decision Dossier keeps the caveat with the claim.

What it asks

A named owner, corridor, decision, and next step—rather than an open-ended map of complaints.

What it shows

Rates, uncertainty, report patterns, exposure quality, and independent evidence as separate, inspectable tracks.

What it refuses

Unqualified safety claims, individual trip disclosure, cross-city leaderboards, and false before-and-after certainty.

Reviewed national fatal-crash reference data

The Conflict Atlas is a separately scoped reference surface: it presents reviewed 2020–2024 NHTSA FARS fatal-crash counts by state and involved road-user mode. It is not a near-miss map and does not estimate local risk.

Read the label

Counts are not risk. The Atlas does not normalize for trips, miles, population, or exposure. Modes can overlap within a crash.
